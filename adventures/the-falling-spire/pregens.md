# The Falling Spire — Pre-generated Characters

Three **level-20** heroes, a balanced trio: a wall, a hunter, a seer. Each is built straight off the progression ladder ([rules/03-progression.md](../../rules/03-progression.md)) — five abilities with all **6 ability bumps** applied (cap `+5`), **2 signatures + all 6 perks + the level-20 capstone**, **master-tier training bonus `+4`**, and **master-tier gear**.

> **How the level-20 math was run, per hero:**
> - **Abilities:** `+3/+2/+1/+1/+0`, +1 class, +1 ancestry, then 6 bumps (levels 2/5/8/11/14/17), capped at `+5`.
> - **HP:** armor-tier base + Might, then `+1d6+Might` per level ×19 (averaged, 1s/2s count as 3).
> - **Perks:** both rungs (basic + advanced) of all 3 components — unlocked by level 18.
> - **Breadth (levels 4/7/10/13/16/19):** martials gained 6 trainings (3 → 9); the caster gained 6 spells (3 → 9).
> - **Capstone (20):** the Class Signature ascends.
> - **Training bonus `+4`** (master tier, 15–20). **Attack ceiling `d20+9`** — frozen, as designed.

## ⚔️ VORRIK FROST-SCALE — *Frost Dragonborn Knight (he/him)*

> A drake-blooded oathkeeper, last of a fallen mountain order. He plants himself between the world and the thing trying to end it, and does not move.

**Class** Knight (#9) — Medium + Heavy weapons · Heavy armor · primary **Might**
**Ancestry** Frost Dragonborn — `+1` Might

### Abilities
| Might | Agility | Mind | Spirit | Charisma |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **+5** | +1 | +2 | +4 | +3 |

*(Base +3 Might/+2 Spirit/+1 Agi/+1 Cha/+0 Mind → +5 Might at L1 already; 6 bumps poured into the mental defenses so he can't be controlled: Spirit +2→+4, Charisma +1→+3, Mind +0→+2.)*

### Vitals
- **HP 186** · Speed 6 · **Training Bonus +4**
- **Armor 23** *(10 + Might 5 + Daedric plate 8)* — or **26** if he swaps to longsword + tower shield
- **Mind 12 · Spirit 14 · Charisma 13**
- Resistance to **cold** (draconic)

### Signatures
- **Racial — Draconic Breath:** 1/rest, exhale a Close 2-square cone; `d20 + 5` vs. each Armor → `2d6` **cold**.
- **Class — Second Wind** → **CAPSTONE (ascended):** as part of any action, regain `2d6 + 20` HP, **twice per rest**.

### Perks (all six)
- **Riposte** + **Riposte Mastery** — when a foe **hits or misses** you in melee (1/round), make a free attack at **advantage**.
- **Cleave** + **Reaving Sweep** — +1d10 on a hit (1/turn); Cleave chains off any hit that drops a foe to 0 **or below half HP**.
- **Bulwark** + **Immovable** — adjacent allies get **+2 Armor**; you can't be moved, knocked Prone, or Grappled against your will.

### Trainings (9, +4)
Athletics · Tactics · Intimidation · Endurance · Perception · Insight · Persuasion · Lore · Religion

### Gear
- **Daedric greatsword** — *Heavy.* Attack **+9** vs. Armor → `1d10 + 10` *(crit: `2d10 + 10`)*
- **Ebony longsword** + **tower shield** (backup turtle set) — Attack **+9** → `1d8 + 9`
- **Daedric plate** (heavy armor) · adventuring kit · 30g
- *Set to Strike:* in plate **and** a heavy weapon, on a turn he moves he takes his **move or attack, not both** — so he holds ground and lets the world come to him.

### How to play Vorrik
Anchor the line. Stand still (full attack, no Set-to-Strike penalty), soak hits, and **Immovable** means knockback/grapple gimmicks bounce off you. **Bulwark +2** hardens the whole party. **Reaving Sweep** turns a crowd into a chain of corpses. Save **Second Wind** (2d6+20, twice!) for when the Oracle can't reach you. Breath is your one ranged answer.

## 🏹 LIRAEL DUSKARROW — *Wood Elf Ranger (she/her)*

> A century-old hunter who has killed three things that were supposed to be unkillable. She marks her quarry, and then it is simply a matter of time.

**Class** Ranger (#3) — Light + Ranged weapons · Light armor · primary **Agility**
**Ancestry** Wood Elf — `+1` Agility

### Abilities
| Might | Agility | Mind | Spirit | Charisma |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| +3 | **+5** | +4 | +3 | +0 |

*(Base +3 Agi/+2 Mind/+1 Might/+1 Spirit/+0 Cha → +5 Agi at L1; 6 bumps: Mind +2→+4, Might +1→+3, Spirit +1→+3.)*

### Vitals
- **HP 125** · Speed 6 · **Training Bonus +4**
- **Armor 20** *(10 + Agility 5 + Dragonscale 5)*
- **Mind 14 · Spirit 13 · Charisma 10**

### Signatures
- **Racial — Keen Senses:** advantage on Perception; see in dim light.
- **Class — Hunter's Mark** → **CAPSTONE (ascended):** mark **up to two** creatures (free, 1/turn); your attacks vs. a marked target ignore cover and deal **+2d6**; re-marks on a kill.

### Perks (all six)
- **Sneak Strike** + **Shadowstrike** — +**2d6** when you attack with advantage or an ally flanks (1/turn); **+3d6** the first time each fight you hit a foe that hasn't acted.
- **Deadeye** + **Piercing Volley** — ignore half-cover, +1d6 at Far (1/turn); once per turn your shot **pierces every creature in a line** and ignores all cover.
- **Slip Away** + **Evasion** — reposition freely when ignored; area effects vs. your Armor deal **half on a hit, none on a miss**.

### Trainings (9, +4)
Acrobatics · Stealth · Perception · Wilderness · Sleight of Hand · Endurance · Athletics · Insight · Medicine

### Gear
- **Daedric longbow** — *Ranged, Far.* Attack **+9** vs. Armor → `1d8 + 10`, **+2d6** vs. a marked target, **+2d6** Shadowstrike, **+1d6** Deadeye at Far. *(A marked, flanked, far target: `1d8 + 10 + 5d6` — roughly **35** on a hit.)*
- **Two Ebony shortswords** — *Light, dual-wield.* Attack **+9** → `1d8 + 9` *(die stepped up)*
- **Dragonscale leather** · **Quiver of Returning** (never runs dry) · adventuring kit · 20g

### How to play Lirael
Open by **marking the two scariest threats** — your damage against them is obscene. Use **Piercing Volley** when enemies line up (a corridor, a charging pack). **Evasion** makes you fearless of the boss's Chain Lightning and breath cones. Stay at **Far** and mobile (**Slip Away**); if something closes, dual swords and disengage. You are the party's burst — focus-fire whatever the Knight is holding.

## 🔮 SERAPHINE VALE — *Protector Aasimar Oracle (she/her)*

> Light leaks from the cracks in her. She has seen how this day can end forty different ways and is here to bend it toward the one where everyone lives.

**Class** Oracle (#43) — Alteration + Destruction + Restoration · no armor · pool **Spirit** (full caster)
**Ancestry** Protector Aasimar — `+1` Spirit

### Abilities
| Might | Agility | Mind | Spirit | Charisma |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| +0 | +3 | +4 | **+5** | +3 |

*(Base +3 Spirit/+2 Mind/+1 Agi/+1 Cha/+0 Might → +5 Spirit at L1; 6 bumps: Mind +2→+4, Agility +1→+3, Charisma +1→+3.)*

### Vitals
- **HP 82** · Speed 6 · **Training Bonus +4** · **Mana 28** *(20 + Spirit 5 + Sunstaff 3)*
- **Armor 13** *(10 + Agility 3)* → **16** while she has mana (**Greater Ward**)
- **Mind 14 · Spirit 15 · Charisma 13**
- Resistance to **radiant and necrotic** (celestial)

### Signatures
- **Racial — Celestial Ward:** resistance to radiant and necrotic.
- **Class — Foresight** → **CAPSTONE (ascended):** **twice per round**, force any creature in sight to reroll a d20; you choose which result stands.

### Perks (all six)
- **Arcane Ward** + **Greater Ward** — **+3 Armor** while you have 1+ mana; 1/rest extend a `+3` ward to a touched ally for 1 min.
- **Devastation** + **Overload** — reroll 1s/2s on Destruction damage (1/turn); 1/rest **maximize** every die of one Destruction spell.
- **Divine Touch** + **Greater Mending** — touch-heal `2d6 + 5` at **Near** range, **6×/rest**.

### Spells — *2 cantrips + 9 known*, cast from Spirit (spell attack **+9**)
- **Cantrips:** Spark · Guidance
- **Restoration:** Cure Wounds (1) · Mass Healing Word (3) · **Heal** (5) · Bless (1)
- **Destruction:** Fireball (3) · Lightning Bolt (3)
- **Alteration:** Shield (1, reaction) · Haste (3) · **Wall of Force** (5)

### Trainings (3, +4)
*(Casters grow spells, not skills.)* Arcana · Religion · Medicine

### Gear
- **Sunstaff** (focus; `+3` mana) · robes (no armor) · Iron dagger · holy symbol · adventuring kit · 15g

### How to play Seraphine
You are control, healing, and a finisher all in one — but you're fragile (**82 HP, Armor 16**), so stay behind Vorrik. **Foresight (twice/round)** is your masterstroke: cancel the boss's recharge attack, force a crit into a miss, or save a downed ally — *twice a round.* **Wall of Force** splits the battlefield; **Haste** the Ranger; **Overload + Fireball** for a guaranteed 36 to a pack. Between fights, **Greater Mending** tops everyone off (6 charges) so you don't burn spell mana on patching. With 28 mana you can afford to be generous — but never empty out before the boss.

## Party at a glance

| | HP | Armor | Mind/Spirit/Cha | Role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Vorrik** (Knight) | 186 | 23–26 | 12 / 14 / 13 | Wall — holds the line, hardens allies, refuses to fall |
| **Lirael** (Ranger) | 125 | 20 | 14 / 13 / 10 | Hunter — mark-and-delete burst, line-piercing shots |
| **Seraphine** (Oracle) | 82 | 13 (16) | 14 / 15 / 13 | Seer — control, heal, fate-bending, blast |

**Coverage check:** a frontline that can't be moved or controlled · a striker that erases priority targets at range · a caster that heals, controls space, blasts, and rewrites dice. No healing gap (Oracle has spells *and* a 6-charge touch heal), no control gap (Wall of Force, Haste, Foresight), no "stuck in melee" gap (everyone has answers). It's a tight, self-sufficient trio.
