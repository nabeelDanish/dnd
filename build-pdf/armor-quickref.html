The two categories

CategoryMaterialArmor formulaCast?
Lightleather / hide10 + Agility + material✅ all casting
Heavymetal / stone10 + Might + material❌ blocks casting
  • No armor: 10 + Agility (+ shield) — the naked glass-cannon classes (buy an extra pick).
  • Untrained armor: disadvantage on Agility & Might rolls and no casting.

⚠ Heavy armor restrictions

  • No ranged weapons while worn — it's the melee line.
  • Set to Strike: move + attack the same turn → attack at disadvantage (with a heavy weapon too → move or attack).
  • Blocks all spellcasting. → Heavy = the planted melee anchor; light/none = mobile.
