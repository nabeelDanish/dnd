# Character Sheet

A fill-in template. The first half is mechanical (what the dice need). The second half is **who they are when the dice aren't rolling** — the part that makes the character feel real at the table.

Fill what you can. Leave the rest blank until play surprises you with the answer.

## IV. Trainings

You have **3** at creation (4 if Human ancestry), and non-casters gain **+1 on each breadth level** (4, 7, 10, 13, 16, 19) — up to ~9–10 by level 20. When a roll touches one of these, add your training bonus.

```
1. ________________   4. ________________   7. ________________
2. ________________   5. ________________   8. ________________
3. ________________   6. ________________   9. ________________
                     10. ________________  (Human / high level)
```
