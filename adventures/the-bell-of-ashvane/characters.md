# Pre-Made Characters

Four level-1 characters, ready to play *The Bell of Ashvane*. Each one is one of the four classes. Each has a hook into the adventure.

**How to use:** print or copy a sheet for each player. The personality and hook sections are roleplaying suggestions, not rules — players are free to ignore them.

---

## 1. Korrad Stoneheart — Dwarf Warrior

> *A broad, weathered dwarf in dented chain. He carries a longsword that has seen too many battles. His eyes are slow but never miss.*

### Stats
- **Might** `+4` *(base +2, +1 Warrior, +1 Dwarf)*
- **Agility** `+1`
- **Mind** `+0`
- **Spirit** `+3`

### Vitals
- **HP:** 14
- **Armor:** 16 *(10 + 1 Agility (capped) + 3 chain shirt + 2 shield)*
- **Mind defense:** 10
- **Spirit defense:** 13
- **Speed:** 6 squares

### Trainings (`+2` bonus)
- **Athletics** *(Warrior)*
- **Tactics** *(Warrior)*
- **Endurance** *(background: disgraced knight)*

### Class features
- **Cleave.** When you reduce a creature to 0 HP with an attack, immediately make another attack as part of the same action against a different target within reach.
- **Bulwark.** While you wear medium or heavy armor, allies adjacent to you have `+1` to their Armor defense.

### Ancestry trait
- **Stone-Hearted** *(Dwarf).* Advantage on Spirit defense rolls against fear and charm. Darkvision 30 ft (you can see in total darkness up to 30 ft).

### Attacks
- **Longsword** (medium, Might): **`+6` vs. Armor → `1d8 + 4`**. *(d20 + 4 Might + 2 training.)*
- **Dagger** (light, Might): **`+6` vs. Armor → `1d4 + 4`**. Range Close, or thrown to Near.

### Gear
- Chain shirt + shield (worn).
- Longsword + dagger.
- Adventuring kit (rope, torches, tinderbox, bedroll, rations, etc.).
- A small silver locket containing a portrait of his late wife (worn under armor).
- 30 gold.

### Background — Disgraced Knight
Once a knight of the Lord-Marshal of Iron Pass. Was stripped of his command after refusing to execute prisoners during a border skirmish. Drinks more than he should. Speaks slowly. Has been a sellsword for fifteen years.

### Personality
- **Loves:** dwarven beer, the locket, telling stories about other people's battles.
- **Fears:** drowning. (He cannot swim.)
- **Hides:** that he is here because the temple paid his way. He is broke.

### Hook into the adventure
A priest of the Sea-Watcher (Father Aldric, see below) recruited him in the inland town and paid passage to Ashvane. Korrad has fought aquatic horrors before — pearl-collar raids on the coast a decade ago. He has seen what a sea hag can do. **He is afraid, and trying not to show it.**

## 2. Brenna Quickfoot — Halfling Scout

> *A small, sharp-eyed halfling in dust-brown leathers. She carries a longbow taller than she is. Her smile shows up before her trust does.*

### Stats
- **Might** `+1`
- **Agility** `+5` *(base +3, +1 Scout, +1 Halfling)*
- **Mind** `+2`
- **Spirit** `+0`

### Vitals
- **HP:** 9
- **Armor:** 16 *(10 + 5 Agility + 1 leather)*
- **Mind defense:** 12
- **Spirit defense:** 10
- **Speed:** 6 squares

### Trainings (`+2` bonus)
- **Stealth** *(Scout)*
- **Perception** *(Scout)*
- **Sleight of Hand** *(background: poacher)*

### Class features
- **Sneak Strike.** Once per turn, when you attack with advantage (or with an ally adjacent to your target), deal an extra `1d6` damage on a hit.
- **Slip Away.** When an enemy adjacent to you takes any action that isn't attacking you, you may move up to half your speed without provoking.

### Ancestry trait
- **Lucky** *(Halfling).* Once per rest, reroll any one d20 of yours and take the new result.

### Attacks
- **Longbow** (ranged, Agility): **`+7` vs. Armor → `1d8 + 5`**, Range Far.
  - *(d20 + 5 Agility + 2 training in ranged.)*
- **Dagger** (light, Agility): **`+7` vs. Armor → `1d4 + 5`**.
- **Thrown dagger:** same, Range Near.

### Gear
- Leather armor (worn).
- Longbow + 20 arrows.
- Two daggers.
- Thieves' tools (`+2` from Sleight of Hand training).
- Adventuring kit.
- A signet ring (stolen, hidden in her boot — worth 50g but recognizable).
- 12 gold.

### Background — Poacher
Hunted in a noble's private forest. Got caught stealing from his lodge. Fled, joined a smuggling crew that ran goods up and down the coast for a year before getting tired of looking over her shoulder. **Knew Ren Halloway**, one of the smugglers named in this adventure, from that season. They were never friends, but they shared more than one cold night on the rocks.

### Personality
- **Loves:** the open road, baked apples, finding things people thought were lost.
- **Fears:** cages. Manacles. Anything that limits her exits.
- **Hides:** the signet ring. The smuggler year. Most of her past, honestly.

### Hook into the adventure
When she heard the village of Ashvane was looking for help with a stolen bell, and the name "Ren Halloway" came up in the tavern gossip, she signed on. She wants to see Ren — alive or dead. **She does not yet know that he is no longer a man.**

## 3. Sylvane Mistwood — Elf Mage

> *A tall, slim elf with ink-stained fingers and bright, curious eyes. She wears traveling robes and carries a small leather-bound book chained to her belt.*

### Stats
- **Might** `+0`
- **Agility** `+2` *(base +1, +1 Elf)*
- **Mind** `+4` *(base +3, +1 Mage)*
- **Spirit** `+2`

### Vitals
- **HP:** 6
- **Armor:** 12 *(10 + 2 Agility, no armor)*
- **Mind defense:** 14
- **Spirit defense:** 12
- **Speed:** 6 squares

### Trainings (`+2` bonus)
- **Arcana** *(Mage)*
- **Investigation** *(Mage)*
- **Lore** *(background: failed apprentice)*

### Class features
- **Spellcasting.** You know 3 spells (below) and 2 cantrips. Your mana pool is `1 + 4 (Mind) = 5` mana per rest. Cantrips are free.
- **Arcane Recovery.** Once per fight, when you roll your spell attack die, you may reroll a `1`.

### Ancestry trait
- **Keen Senses** *(Elf).* Advantage on Perception rolls. You can see clearly in dim light.

### Cantrips
- **Spark.** Spell attack `+6` vs. Armor → `1d6` fire, Range Near. *(d20 + 4 Mind + 2 Arcana training.)*
- **Daze.** Spell attack `+6` vs. Mind → target **Stunned** until end of their next turn, Range Near.

### Spells known
- **Magic Missile** (1 mana). Auto-hit. Three darts, `1d4 + 1` each. Range Far.
- **Shield** (1 mana, reaction). When you are hit, add `+5` to your Armor until your next turn. May retroactively turn a hit into a miss.
- **Burning Hands** (1 mana). 3-square cone. `+6` vs. Armor against each creature → `2d6` fire on hit.

### Attacks (non-spell)
- **Dagger** (light, Agility — trained): **`+4` vs. Armor → `1d4 + 2`**.

### Gear
- Traveling robes.
- Dagger.
- Spellbook (chained to belt).
- Adventuring kit.
- Component pouch (free, decorative — material components not tracked).
- A small piece of sea-glass she picked up on a beach years ago. (Coincidence. Maybe.)
- 18 gold.

### Background — Failed apprentice
Studied at the Cresselin Academy for forty years. Was expelled at her last review for unauthorized research into binding rituals — she had been trying to bind a small water spirit, and almost succeeded. The Academy considered this *both* dangerous and underwhelming. Has been on the road for three years since, looking for places where her chosen specialty actually matters.

### Personality
- **Loves:** knowledge. Properly cataloged information. Libraries. The specific kind of silence that comes after a question is answered.
- **Fears:** failure. Being seen as unwise. The Academy was right.
- **Hides:** the partial binding ritual she still works on in her notebook. If she could finish it, she could prove them wrong.

### Hook into the adventure
She has been studying coastal magical phenomena. When she heard about a *bell that rings wrong*, she put down her tea and started walking. **She suspects, before anyone else does, that the wrecker is some species of bound water spirit.** She might recognize Marraghan for what she is the moment she sees her.

## 4. Father Aldric Vey — Human Priest

> *A solid, salt-and-pepper man in a chain shirt and a faded blue stole. He carries a mace and a holy symbol in the shape of a lighthouse. He smiles like he means it, but his eyes have officiated too many funerals.*

### Stats
- **Might** `+3` *(base +2, +1 Human)*
- **Agility** `+0`
- **Mind** `+1`
- **Spirit** `+4` *(base +3, +1 Priest)*

### Vitals
- **HP:** 11
- **Armor:** 13 *(10 + 0 Agility + 3 chain shirt)*
- **Mind defense:** 11
- **Spirit defense:** 14
- **Speed:** 6 squares

### Trainings (`+2` bonus)
- **Religion** *(Priest)*
- **Medicine** *(Priest)*
- **Insight** *(background: temple acolyte)*
- **Persuasion** *(Human "Adaptable" trait)*

### Class features
- **Spellcasting.** You know 3 spells and 2 cantrips. Your mana pool is `1 + 4 (Spirit) = 5` mana per rest. Cantrips are free.
- **Divine Touch.** As your action, touch a creature: heal them for `1d6 + 4` HP. Free, usable `5` times per rest (`1 + Spirit`).

### Ancestry trait
- **Adaptable** *(Human).* You picked a fourth training (Persuasion, above).

### Cantrips
- **Sacred Flame.** Spell attack `+6` vs. Spirit → `1d8` radiant, Range Near. Target glows faintly (negates Hide/Invisibility) until end of their next turn.
- **Guidance.** Touch a willing creature; their next ability check within 1 minute is made with **advantage**.

### Spells known
- **Cure Wounds** (1 mana). Touch one creature, heal `2d8 + 4` HP.
- **Bless** (1 mana, ongoing). Up to three allies in Near range; each has **advantage** on their next attack roll.
- **Healing Word** (1 mana). One creature at Far range; heal `1d6 + 4` HP. Can wake a downed ally.

### Attacks
- **Mace** (medium, Might): **`+5` vs. Armor → `1d8 + 3`**.
- **Light crossbow** (ranged, Agility — *not trained*): **`+0` vs. Armor → `1d6`**. Range Far. He carries it but he is terrible with it.

### Gear
- Chain shirt (worn).
- Mace, light crossbow + 20 bolts.
- Holy symbol (a small silver lighthouse on a chain).
- Adventuring kit.
- A flask of consecrated oil (one-use: throw at undead, `1d6` radiant + Burning).
- A copy of the *Litany of the Watchful* (his temple's prayer book — water-stained).
- 20 gold.

### Background — Temple acolyte
A priest of the Sea-Watcher, a coastal faith that maintains lighthouses, signal-bells, and shrines along the western shore. Took his vows at twenty; has served the inland temple at Bridghaven (a day's coach from Ashvane) for fifteen years. **Knows Bell-keeper Elsa personally** — they trained together as novices. He has officiated too many funerals after wrecks like this one.

### Personality
- **Loves:** strong tea. Sermons that say something. His older brother, who is a fisherman three towns south.
- **Fears:** arriving too late.
- **Hides:** a creeping doubt. The Sea-Watcher promises mercy for the drowned. He no longer believes it.

### Hook into the adventure
Elsa sent for him. He arrived this morning on the same coach as the rest of the party, and learned the details on the road. **He is the only one who knows Elsa personally,** and the others may turn to him when she is too frantic to make sense.
