# The Forge of Fury — Image-Generation Prompts

Copy-paste prompts for an image model (ChatGPT/DALL·E, Midjourney, etc.) — the mountain, the descent, the tenants of Khundrukar, and the climax. *(No player-character art; bring your own party.)* Plain-description prompts work in ChatGPT; the `--ar`/`--v` flags are for Midjourney.

## Shared style (prepend or append to every prompt for a consistent look)

> *Painterly cinematic fantasy concept art. A ruined dwarven hold in the roots of a cold northern mountain — vast subterranean halls of hewn black granite, dwarven pillars and defaced carvings, forge-smoke and cold running water, deep shadow lit by embers, torchlight, and the pale blue of darkvision. Muted stone greys and iron-blues shot through with hot forge-orange and, recurring, the light-drinking black of Durgeddin's steel. Grim, ancient, monumental. High detail, fantasy film key-art.*

**Tips:** keep that style line identical across the set so they read as one book. Each prompt sets its own lighting (grey daylight at the gate, ember-glow in the forge, cold dark at the lake) — leave the rest of the style line untouched. For Midjourney reuse one `--seed`; in ChatGPT say *"same style and palette as the last image."*

## The mountain & the descent (establishing shots)

### 1 · Stonehome Mountain & the dwarf-road
> Sweeping establishing shot of a cold, bleak northern mountain under a grey overcast sky, an old cracked dwarven road climbing toward a dark arched gate cut into the mountain's stony root, pines and scree, a thin cold waterfall spilling from the gate-mouth. Ancient, abandoned, foreboding — a hold that has changed hands a dozen bloody times. Epic wide shot, muted greys and greens, thin daylight. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

### 2 · The Mountain Door — the chasm bridge
> The entrance hall of a ruined dwarven hold: a great arched gate with hacked, defaced dwarven faces above it, and just inside, a black chasm splitting the floor forty feet down to cold water, spanned by a single sagging rope-and-plank bridge. A cold stream pours through and off into the gorge. An orc guard-fire flickers on the far side; a warhorn hangs on the wall. Grey daylight from the gate fading into torch-lit dark. Tense, vertiginous. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

### 3 · The Glitterhame
> A vast natural limestone cavern deep under the mountain, wet walls glittering with a thousand points of mineral light where torchlight catches them, drifts of pale glowing mushrooms, black water threading everywhere downhill into the dark. Dwarven-cut passages break into the raw cave at the edges. Beautiful and eerie, cold blues and faint bioluminescent greens against black stone. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

### 4 · The Sinkhole
> A black flooded cavern deep in the mountain, a subterranean river filling it nearly wall to wall, a narrow ledge of wet stone spires picked out by failing torchlight, the dark swallowing everything beyond. Dripping, cold, claustrophobic — one of the stone spires, almost unnoticeable, has a single baleful eye. Deep blue-black, glistening wet stone, a lurking dread. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

### 5 · The Foundry — Durgeddin's forge
> The heart of a ruined dwarven stronghold: an enormous forge-hall of black granite, a row of cold furnaces down one wall with two coughing fresh red fire, racks of half-finished black blades, a great anvil the size of a cart beneath a dark chimney, a stone water-channel with a flat barge, and a wall of dwarven machinery — great chained sluice-wheels and a carved cross-section diagram. Hot forge-orange bleeding into cold shadow, monumental and sorrowful. Wide interior shot. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

### 6 · The Black Lake
> An enormous half-drowned cavern at the very bottom of a dwarven hold: a vast black mirror of still water under a ceiling lost in darkness, a shore of wet gravel and old bones, acid-pitted walls, and glints across the water where a dragon's hoard lies half-sunk. On a rock shelf above the waterline, a dark coiled shape. Cold, vast, silent, menacing. Deep blacks and iron-blues, a single shaft of dim light. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

## The tenants of Khundrukar

### 7 · Great Ulfe, the orc chieftain
> Portrait of a monstrous half-ogre orc chieftain, near ogre-sized, slab-muscled, grey-green hide scarred and tattooed, one tusk capped in crude gold, sprawled on a broken dwarven throne meant for a lord half his size. Across his knees rests a great black-bladed axe that drinks the firelight and gives none back — the finest weapon in the mountain, and he treats it like a lucky charm. Firepit glow, filthy furs, brutal and vain. *[style]* `--ar 2:3 --v 6`

### 8 · Yorrin Ashfell, the captured antiquarian *(the party's key — play him warm)*
> Portrait of a soot-stained, scab-kneed dwarf antiquarian in his middle years, caged behind lashed stalagmites in a wet glittering cavern, a wry frightened grin, bright clever eyes, satchels of notes and lore-scraps still strapped to him. Fearless about the dark and the orcs — but flinching from the running water at his feet. Talkative, likable, terrified, and about to become the most useful person the party meets. Cool cavern blue-green, one warm torch. *[style]* `--ar 2:3 --v 6`

### 9 · The troglodytes
> A pack of hunched cave-dwelling troglodytes — grey-green scaled reptilian humanoids with lashing tails, clawed hands, and cold flat eyes — clustered in a foul den in a dark limestone cavern, hissing, cringing from the light, a rank haze of stench almost visible around them. In their midst a wizened trog mystic with crude bone-charms and a raised, glowing hand. Cowardly, savage, photophobic. Cold cave-dark, sickly greens. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

### 10 · Xazandra, the duergar overseer
> Portrait of a gray dwarf (duergar) overseer — ash-grey skin, colorless eyes, a bald head and a joyless, flat expression — in dark utilitarian mail, directing a salvage crew crating black blades in a forge-hall. A war-pick at her belt, a ledger-slate in one hand, utterly transactional and unimpressed. Not a fanatic; a negotiator who'd sell anyone to anyone. Ember-orange light on grey skin, cold shadow behind. *[style]* `--ar 2:3 --v 6`

### 11 · Durgeddin's Shade *(the tragic core — grief, not menace)*
> Portrait of the translucent, ghostly shade of an ancient dwarven master-smith, broad and powerful in a scorched leather forge-apron, a spectral hammer raised over a cold anvil, striking it endlessly with no spark at all. His bearded face is not fierce but hollowed with two hundred years of grief — the look of a man who has forgotten how to stop. Ghost-blue and ember-orange, sorrowful, monumental, heartbreaking rather than frightening. *[style]* `--ar 2:3 --v 6`

Faint cold light glimmers along its edge. Beautiful, deadly, freighted with grief. Ember-orange rim-light on light-devouring black metal. *[style]* `--ar 3:2 --v 6`

## The Black Lake (the boss & climax)

### 13 · Nightscale, the young black dragon
> A young black dragon coiled on a rock shelf above a vast black subterranean lake, plunder heaped beneath her — sleek wet-coal scales, a single backswept horn-crest, cruel amber eyes, acid steaming faintly at the corners of her fanged mouth, water sheeting off her as she lifts her head to regard an intruder. Sleek, vain, greedy, and dangerous — but young, with a flicker of old fear when she sees black steel. Cold blue-black cavern, one shaft of dim light on wet scales. *[style]* `--ar 2:3 --v 6`

### 14 · Acid Breath *(the boss in action)*
> A young black dragon surging up out of a black underground lake in an explosion of spray, jaws wide, spewing a raking line of sizzling green-black acid across a gravel shore, adventurers scattering before it, the hoard glinting behind. Dynamic, terrifying, kinetic. Deep blacks and iron-blue water lit by the sickly green glow of the acid and scattered torch-fire. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

### 15 · The lake drained — Nightscale stranded *(the win-button pays off)*
> A young black dragon floundering, humiliated and furious, on a wide expanse of black mud and old bones — a great cavern lake suddenly drained to mudflats, dwarven sluice-water still draining away, her element gone. She rears, no longer able to dive and hide, cornered by figures with light-drinking black blades. Cold grey mud, draining channels, a dragon out of water. Dramatic, decisive. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

## Resolution

### 16 · The forge goes quiet *(the shade at rest)*
> A ruined dwarven forge-hall finally silent and still, the furnaces guttering to cold embers, a great spectral hammer laid to rest on a cold anvil, the ghostly shade of the old smith fading gently into peace at last — a faint dwarvish blessing-light kindling briefly along a black blade left on the anvil. Mournful, tender, resolved. Fading ember-orange into soft dark, a single clean point of cold light. *[style]* `--ar 16:9 --v 6`

### 17 · The black blades brought home *(reward)*
> Close-up on a caravan-master's plain plank table in warm daylight at the mountain's foot: three or four legendary black dwarven blades laid out on rough cloth beside a spill of old gold coin and a pair of uncut gems, an antiquarian dwarf's ink-stained hand resting on one. The last work of a doomed smith, finally carried out into the light. Warm daylight after all the dark, muted greys and gold, quietly triumphant. *[style]* `--ar 3:2 --v 6`
